A Little Bit of Background

I love spicy food as mentioned on my homepage. I developed this recipe so no body would want to touch my food. If your a spice addict like me then you'll enjoy this recpie.
Warning: Do NOT make if you're sensitive to Capsaisan

Ingredients

  • One Pound of Hamburger
  • Two Cans of Tomato Sauce
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Italian Seasioning
  • Garlic Powder
  • Chili Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • The Hottest Hot Sauce You can Handle
  • Noodles

The Process

  1. Brown the hamburger.
  2. Drian the fat of you like.
  3. Pour in you tomato sauce.
  4. Season to taste
  5. Add your hot sauce carfully.
  6. Boil your noodles.
  7. Strain, plate, and enjoy
